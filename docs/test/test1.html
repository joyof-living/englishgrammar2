에스보냥 (SVOCAt) QA 테스트 페이지

테스트 방법: 각 문장을 드래그(마우스로 선택)하면 "📖 문법 분석" 버튼이 뜹니다. 버튼을 클릭해 사이드패널에서 결과를 확인하세요.
좌측 색상 막대: ● 쉬움 / ● 중간 / ● 어려움

1. 단순 문장 (S+V+O) — 기본 동작 확인

CNN
The Trump administration has gutted US aid for family planning.
기대: S(The Trump administration) + V(has gutted) + O(US aid) + A(for family planning)
Classic
The cat chased the mouse.
기대: S + V + O 3청크

2. 축약형 (it's, won't, they've, etc.) — 확장 분리 검증

CNN
It's a Game of Thrones type of atmosphere.
기대: S(it) + V(is) 분리, "it's" 통째로 안 나와야 함
Classic
She won't attend the meeting because she's still recovering.
기대: won't → will + not 분리, she's → she + is 분리
Classic
I'd have told you if I'd known earlier.
기대: I'd → I + would/had (맥락 기반 선택), 2번 등장

3. 직접 인용문 — RULE D 검증

Classic
He said, "Wow!"
기대: "Wow!"는 O로 분류 (interjection 아님)
Classic (확장 개발 중 주요 테스트 케이스)
Sarandos said that while "it is very unusual for a founder to step away from the board of the company after succession," Hastings is "no ordinary founder."
기대: O가 전체 that-절로 번들. 드릴다운에서 내부 분해
Classic
"Help!" she cried as the door slammed shut.
기대: "Help!"는 fronted O, adverb clause "as..."

4. 관계절 (who/which/that) — BUNDLING 검증

CNN
Ukrainian infantry officer Oleksiy Mykhailov, who spent 343 days without leaving the front line, underscores Kyiv's manpower issues.
기대: S가 "Ukrainian...front line"까지 (관계절 포함 통째 번들)
CNN
Surveillance footage shows the wolf, which went missing after escaping from its enclosure in O-World zoo.
기대: O가 "the wolf, which...zoo" 통째 번들

5. 분사구문 — 도입부 부사 역할 검증

Classic
Walking down the street, he noticed a suspicious figure.
기대: A(Walking down the street) + S(he) + V(noticed) + O
Classic
Exhausted from the long journey, the travelers fell asleep immediately.
기대: 분사구문이 A로, 드릴다운에서 소문자 v/o/a

6. 부사절 (because/while/although/if) — RULE C Case 3 검증

CNN
Hundreds of Everest hopefuls are waiting at base camp as glacier blocks route to summit.
기대: A(as glacier...summit)를 드릴다운하면 conj(as) + S/V/O 분해
Classic
Although it was raining, the children continued to play outside.
기대: A(Although it was raining)로 시작하는 구조

7. that-절 보어 — 발화/인지 동사 번들링 검증

CNN
Trump says he will extend ceasefire with Iran until negotiations conclude.
기대: O가 "he will extend...conclude" 전체 (implicit that)
Classic
Scientists believe that climate change is accelerating faster than previously predicted.
기대: O가 "that climate change...predicted" 전체 번들. 드릴다운 시 conj(that) + S/V/C

8. 수동태 — tense/voice 표시 검증

CNN
The strategic Strait of Hormuz was again closed on April 19 in the standoff between Iran and the United States.
기대: V 드릴다운 시 voice: "passive" 표시
Classic
The cake was eaten by the children.
기대: V(was eaten) voice: passive, A(by the children)

9. 도치·강조 구문 — 어려운 구조

Classic
Not only did he finish the project, but he also received a promotion.
기대: 도치 구조 인식, conj(but) 연결 두 절
Classic
Never before have we seen such a rapid change in technology.
기대: "Never before"는 A로, 도치된 V(have seen) 인식

10. 긴 복문 — 뉴스 통합 스트레스 테스트

CNN 스타일
A California company has recalled more than 3.1 million bottles of lubricating eye drops because it had not properly tested – and thus could not prove – whether the products were sterile.
기대: 메인 S/V/O + 긴 A(because절) + 내부 부사구. 드릴다운 시 점진적 분해
Classic + news
The committee announced that, despite months of negotiations, the proposal had been rejected by a narrow margin, leaving the future of the initiative uncertain.
기대: that-절 번들, 내부에 despite 구, passive, 분사구 "leaving..."

11. 단어 사전 모드 — 단일 단어 선택 검증

Single word
serendipity
기대: 문장 모드 대신 단어 사전 카드가 표시됨 (word-result 상태)
Single word
ubiquitous
기대: 발음 + 품사 + 한국어 뜻 표시
